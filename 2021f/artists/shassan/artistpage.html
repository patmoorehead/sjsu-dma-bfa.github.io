Suhayla Hassan
"Digital Lobe"
Artist Statement
My primary focus of work is to give insight into the human psyche in a world with increased consumption of technology and mass media using digital mediums and her own experiences. I conceptually focus on the relationship shared between the real world and the virtual world where our devices are a link between the two. Utilizing technology to rethinking older ideas and concepts of previous artists and scholars through a new lens. In my most recent projects, I approach society’s growing dependency on technology and its disruption to people’s functionality and how people view themselves. Disruptions such as anxieties, altered memory, disrupted identities, and a sense of reality. I often show this disruption using a “glitch aesthetic” in combination with 3D models or Video.
