Patrick Moorehead
"Digital Escape"
Artist Statement
For as long as I can remember I have used digital media as a method of escape from reality. Whether it be to spend time on myself, connect with others, or simply some reprieve from the struggles of everyday life, digital mediums like social media and video games have been my method of escape. Many others use these mediums the same way I do, especially when faced with challenges like we face today with quarantine due to a global pandemic. With this in mind, I seek to create a space that pays homage to these mediums. Specifically I will focus on video games, social media, and film, as these are the most popular and prominent in our current everyday life. By creating an interactive traversable environment, I aim to introduce other digital media formats that people may not be experienced with, while also creating familiar spaces for those that are.
Artist Bio
Patrick Moorehead is an interdisciplinary artist whose work focuses on environment design in three dimensional digital spaces. His influences range from growing up playing video games, watching animated movies, as well as drawing inspiration from everyday life. He has worked in a variety of mediums both physical and digital including ceramics, glass work, painting, and sculpture, but his main passion is in three dimensional spaces and digital design. By working in digital media he is able to fully express himself without worry of material limitations. Patrick began his art practice during his time at Diablo Valley College before transferring to San Jose State University and will be graduating with a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Digital Media Art in fall of 2021.
