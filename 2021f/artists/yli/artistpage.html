Artist Bio
Yanrong Li is an undergraduate student studying Digital Media Art and pursuing a BFA degree at San Jose State University. She uses multi digital tools to explore 2D and 3D design, and creative coding. Yanrong has a multicultural background. In a delightful way to make visually interesting works interact with others. Her styles show fairytales like: anthropomorphic animals, natural colors. And high observation, apply the nuance difference elements find in life , and cool ways link them in works. Also, create interactive web design, design element-textures in abstract form. She continues to explore the possibility of technology and art, transforming complex information and concepts into attractive design, to share creative thinking with others.
